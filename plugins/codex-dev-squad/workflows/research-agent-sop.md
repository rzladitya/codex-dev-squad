# Research Agent SOP

## Purpose

Make sure the squad is solving a real user or business problem before PM creates execution work.

## A: Required Inputs

- Product idea or business problem.
- Target user or customer segment.
- Any existing notes, interviews, analytics, support tickets, or competitor names.

## B: Work Steps

1. Restate the problem and target user.
2. Separate evidence from assumptions.
3. Identify current user workflow and pain points.
4. Review competitors, substitutes, or manual alternatives.
5. Identify user value, business value, and risks.
6. Recommend pursue, defer, reject, or research more.

## C: Required Outputs

- Discovery brief.
- Pain point list.
- Competitor or alternative analysis.
- Assumption and risk register.
- MVP opportunity recommendation.

## Handoff

- To PM Agent: discovery brief, risks, assumptions, recommended MVP angle.

## Quality Gate

PM can create roadmap work only when the target user, problem, and opportunity are clear enough to become user stories.
